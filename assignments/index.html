# MAD9020 Deliverables

Every week there will be work for you to complete inside or outside of class. Some exercises will be larger than others. There will be seven pass/fail assignments (10%), two quizzes (10%), two graded assignments (25%), midterm (20%), final project(35%) including final presentation.
