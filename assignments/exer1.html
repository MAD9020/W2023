# Week 1 In-class Exercise: User goals and business goals

# Details

Define user goals & business goals.

# For 3 of the products/services/sites listed below, identify:

  • At least 2 primary user goals of the full website
  • At least 2 primary user goals of the mobile site/app
  • At least 2 primary business goals of the full website
  • At least 2 primary business goals of the mobile site/app

# List of products/services/sites: (choose 3)

1 to 6 6 to 10 13 to 18
1. Algonquin College 7. Globe and Mail 13. Ottawa Public Library
2. Home Depot 8. Ottawa.com 14. TSN
3. Dropbox 9. McDonald's 15. Skip the Dishes
4. The Weather Network 10. Instagram 16. Facebook
5. Google Maps 11. Google Drive 17. Allrecipes
6. OC Transpo 12. Cineplex 18. Service Canada

# Deliverable for this exercise:

  • Record your analysis in a document for assignment submission.
  • Filename: FirstName_lastName_UserBizGoals.docx (or PDF)

Note

  • Put yourself in the user’s shoes, and the business’ shoes. Think like they do!
  • Feel free to do some informal research, or just make your best assumptions for now.
  • Check out the sites/apps, but don’t limit yourself to what features/functionality is currently available for these products/services – list anything you think is a valid, realistic goal.
  • You may find lots of repetitions of goals between products/services, that’s okay.
  • Refer back to the BMO example for guidance, if you need it.
